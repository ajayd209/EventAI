Simple Process

How It Works

From idea to published event plan in just five simple steps.

1
Enter Event Details

Provide your event name, type, expected attendance, venue and budget. Takes less than 2 minutes.

2
AI Generates Complete Plan

Our Gemini AI creates a full event plan including schedule, budget breakdown, resources, tasks and risk analysis.

3
Review and Edit

Read through the generated plan, make any changes you need, and save it as a draft if you're not ready yet.

4
Publish Your Event

When you're satisfied with the plan, publish it to finalize and make it part of your event portfolio.

5
Manage from My Plans

Access all your published and draft plans, track tasks, view history and generate reports anytime.
